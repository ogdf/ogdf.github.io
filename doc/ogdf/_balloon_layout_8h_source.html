48 /*
49  * The layout is computed by first computing a spanning tree
50  * of the graph that is then used to derive the vertices' coordinates.
51  * First, the radii at each vertex are computed.
52  * Then, depending on the embedding option, the order of the
53  * edges around each vertex is optimized to maximize angular
54  * resolution and to minimize the aspect ratio.
55  *
56  * Finally, the layout is shifted into the positive quadrant
57  * of the cartesian plane
58  * */
